Dr. Muhammad Iqbal

Regional Director

Bio

Dr. Muhammad Iqbal is English Programs Regional Manager at the U. S. Consulate General in Lahore for the last more than 14 years. He funds, oversees, and collaborates with the partner organizations for English language programs in the Punjab province. He is a PhD in Linguistics. Before joining the Consulate, he taught at the University of Punjab, Allama lqbal Open University, National University of Modern Languages, and Lahore Leads University. Dr. Iqbal has more than 24-year teaching and teacher training experience.

Prof. Dr. M. Asad Meraj

Principal

Bio

Prof. Dr. Muhammad Asad Meraj is the Principal of the University of Education, Multan Campus, and a Professor of Mathematics. He earned his PhD in Mathematics from Karl-Franzens Universität Graz, Austria. Since joining UE Multan on 14th February 2020, he has focused on raising academic standards, promoting research, and improving campus administration. Known for his disciplined leadership and student-centered approach, he continues to contribute to the growth and excellence of the institution.

M.Farhan Rafi

Program Manager

Bio

Farhan Rafi is an English language trainer, freelance writer, and academic from Multan with over 20 years of teaching experience. He holds a Master’s in English and an MS in Linguistics. A key milestone in his journey was joining the U.S.-sponsored English Access Program in 2012, followed by a Cultural Immersion experience in the U.S. in 2013. Currently, he serves as Program Manager at the University of Education Lahore, Multan Campus, where he promotes modern teaching methods and inspires youth with values of tolerance, peace, and leadership.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive our educational approach and commitment to student success.

Our Mission

Access Class is dedicated to providing high-quality English language education to talented students from underserved communities. We aim to foster linguistic proficiency, cultural understanding, and critical thinking skills that empower students to succeed in an increasingly interconnected world.

  • Provide accessible, high-quality English language education
  • Foster cross-cultural understanding and global citizenship
  • Develop critical thinking and creative expression skills
  • Create pathways to educational and professional opportunities

Our Vision

We envision a world where all students, regardless of socioeconomic background, have access to transformative educational experiences that prepare them for success in a global society. Our vision is to be a catalyst for positive change through language education and cultural exchange.

  • Bridge educational opportunity gaps in underserved communities
  • Create a global network of engaged, multilingual citizens
  • Inspire lifelong learning and cross-cultural collaboration
  • Become a model for innovative language education worldwide

Access History

The journey of Access Class from its founding to its current impact on students worldwide.

Program Foundation

2010

Access Class was established with an initial cohort of 30 students and 2 teachers, focusing on providing English language education to talented students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

  • Received initial grant funding to support program development

Program Expansion

2015

The program expanded to serve 150 students across multiple locations, introducing specialized tracks in academic writing, public speaking, and digital media to enhance student skills.

  • Recognized for excellence in educational programming by the International Education Association

Global Partnerships

2018

Access Class established partnerships with international educational institutions, creating exchange opportunities and virtual collaboration projects for students to gain global perspectives.

  • Launched the Global Voices initiative connecting students across 10 countries

Current Impact

2025

Today, Access Class serves over 500 students annually, with a network of 2,000+ alumni who have gone on to pursue higher education and impactful careers. Our comprehensive curriculum now includes specialized tracks in creative expression, digital literacy, and leadership development.

  • Recognized as a leader in innovative language education by UNESCO
